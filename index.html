Lexus Camlock

Smooth & Stable Player Tracking

Lexus Camlock is a script made to lock onto players with smooth and stable tracking. It is built to work on both mobile and PC and gives users simple controls that are easy to use. The goal of Lexus Camlock is to give a clean and reliable lock system without extra features or confusing settings. It is made to run fast, stay consistent, and give users a better experience in any game they use it in.
